Core Activities

The Four Conquering
College Labs

These labs are your starting point. Complete them in order. Each builds directly on the last — and together they form the foundation of everything else.

LAB 01 / 04
📅
Schedule to Succeed

Lab 1: Build Your Academic Schedule

Time is your most precious resource. In this lab you design a weekly schedule and term calendar that balances deep learning with the rest of your life — work, family, rest, and joy.

Weekly Schedule Template — block every hour of your week to protect your deep learning time before distractions fill it.

Term Calendar — map all major deadlines, exams, and personal commitments so nothing catches you off-guard.

50-Year Life Plan — zoom out and ask: where am I going? What role does this class play in my larger story?

SMART Goals — translate big visions into concrete, measurable targets for this specific academic term.

LAB 02 / 04
🔥
Prepare for Deep Learning

Lab 2: Ignite Your Learning

What is deep learning — really? This lab introduces the science of expertise, deliberate practice, and the Sweet Spot where real growth happens. Learn to recognize shallow work and replace it with full ignition.

Sweet Spot Awareness — identify when you're in your comfort zone, survival zone, or true learning zone.

Intrinsic Motivation Map — connect your coursework to the deeper reasons you chose this path.

Five Stages of Deep Learning — a personal map from first exposure to unconscious competence.

Deliberate Practice Plan — structure your study sessions as purposeful practice + corrective feedback loops.

LAB 03 / 04
🗒️
Prepare for Flipped Learning

Lab 3: Create Your Note System

In a flipped classroom, your first contact with content happens before class — not during it. This lab teaches you to build lecture note systems that capture, question, and transform raw information into lasting understanding.

Lecture Rewrite System — a two-pass process that separates what you know from what you need help with.

2-Minute Question Rule — capture specific, timestamped questions that you can bring to class or office hours.

Deep Learning Space Design — optimize where and how you study: lighting, sound, temperature, tools.

Peer-to-Peer Learning Habits — learn how to use your classmates as teachers and become a teacher yourself.

LAB 04 / 04
📂
Create Your Dream Binder

Lab 4: Build Your Dream Binder

A Dream Binder is your external brain — a living document that connects your daily class work to your 5-year vision. It holds your academic plan, scholarship tracker, career goals, and your reasons for doing all this.

5-Year Academic Plan — a course-by-course roadmap to your degree(s), mapped to your life goals.

Second Brain / Capture System — an organized external memory so you never lose an idea, question, or insight.

Scholarship & Opportunity Tracker — identify funding sources and build a 12-month application calendar.

Letter of Recommendation System — a proactive strategy to build relationships with professors long before you need their letter.

Jeff's Principles

Five Rules for Learning in This Class

These aren't punishments or policies. They're invitations — a way of thinking about your work that puts you in control of your growth and your future.

"Learning results from what the student does and thinks, and only from what the student does and thinks." — Herbert A. Simon
01

Health Comes First

If you must choose between your class and your physical, mental, emotional, or spiritual health — choose your health. Always. Reach out proactively so your teacher can support your return.

Non-negotiable
02

Deep Learning > Shallow Learning

Avoid the comfort zone (too easy) and the survival zone (too hard / just hacking). Spend as much time as possible in your sweet spot — where productive struggle leads to real growth.

The Core Practice
03

Show Up. Show Out. Show Joy.

Be present for yourself and your community. Give your best effort. Find moments of genuine joy in the work — even when it's hard, especially when it's hard.

Community Commitment
04

Veggies Before Dessert

Finish the work you committed to before chasing side projects. But dessert is mandatory too — your dreams, your creative work, the things that light you up — those matter.

Productivity
05

Show Your Boss a 20/10

Produce 100+ for yourself. When you exceed expectations in your core responsibilities, you earn the security and freedom to build the work you actually care about. Excellence creates options.
